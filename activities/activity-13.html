“Enshittification”

  1. What is a platform? Consider both who platforms serve and what platforms do?

  2. What is “Enshittification”?

  3. Pick an existing platform and describe what the “Enshittification” process looks like.

  4. What is a “Network Effect”?

  5. What policies allow “Enshittification” to happen?

  6. What needs to happen to prevent it?

  7. Why is this a societal / ethical issue? Consider:
    • What kinds of important human activities do platforms mediate?
  8. How does “enshittification” shape the direction that AI takes? Consider any of the following dimensions:
    • Monetization as the primary goal
    • Bias and discrimination (e.g., using data-driven decision-making to favor groups more likely to generate revenue)
    • Concentration of AI resources towards the goals of very rich and powerful people (business and government interests)
    • Content quality
